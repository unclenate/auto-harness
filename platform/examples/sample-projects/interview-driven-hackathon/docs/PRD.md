# PRD — Hackathon Sample

**Problem.** Hackathon teams need to ship a working prototype in 48 hours; canonical
multi-file PRD ceremony does not fit the available time budget.

**In scope (MVP).** A single product requirements document that names the problem,
the in-scope feature set, the explicit out-of-scope items, and the success metric.

**Out of scope (explicit).** Authentication, multi-tenant isolation, observability,
billing. These are not "later" — they are out of scope for this hackathon entry.

**Success metric.** Judges can complete the demo flow end-to-end without intervention.

**Open questions.** None at PRD time. Raise as ADRs if they emerge during implementation.
